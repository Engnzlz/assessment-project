Software Applications

Is knowledge of the Stork application, including installation, configuration, latest version and navigation. And the ability to assist with application updates and troubleshooting related to the application.

  • Installing the app: Steps to install the Stork app on various devices (iOS and Android).
  • Navigating the app: Guiding users through the app interface, including setting up profiles and accessing health data.
  • Data synchronization: Ensure that Stork sensor data is properly synchronized with the app.
  • Push Notifications: Configure and troubleshoot notifications for health alerts.

Wireless Communication

Wi-Fi: Many Baby Stork monitors connect to your home Wi-Fi network, allowing them to transmit data to the app over the internet.

  • Bluetooth: Some monitors may use Bluetooth for direct connections to smartphones or tablets for localized data sharing.

Operating System

It is the knowledge of different operating systems (such as iOS, Android), and how to find the information on the device.

Data Transmission

Real-Time Data: The monitor captures data such as sound, motion, or vital signs (if applicable) and sends it in real-time to the app.
